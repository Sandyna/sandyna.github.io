We supply hot chocolate for events such as birthday parties, weddings, meetups and more!

The chocolate is delivered in hot chocolate dispensers to keep it hot through the whole event.
Currently, we have four types of chocolate available.


Classic milk chocolate is pretty sweet.
We use milk chocolate with cacao content 37%


Richer, thicker and less sweet chocolate.
The cacao content in the chocolate used is 55%
We recommend semisweet chocolate if you can't decide.


Dark and rich, with the least sugar.
It's also the thickest chocolate we offer. The cacao content in the chocolate we use is 82%


White and very sweet chocolate.
It resembles condensed milk. If you love sweet, this is the chocolate for you.
