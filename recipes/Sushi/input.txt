Sushi rice
30m
5
n/a

2 cups sushi rice
Water (according to instructions on rice)
3 tbsp rice vinegar
2 tbsp sugar
1.5 tsp salt

Cook rice according to instructions on the packaging. If nothing is specified there, use a bit more than 2 cups water per cup of rice. Rinse rice, put together with water.
Cook covered on highest for ~5m, until the rice boils. Reduce heat and boil for 15 more minutes (for a total of 20m). Don't stir, ever. After that, leave covered for 5-10 more minutes.
Prepare rice seasoning: Put vinegar, sugar and salt with a bit of water, heat up and dissolve everything.
Scoop out rice into a bowl, add rice seasoning and mix well. Add seasoning gradually, not to overdo it. Leave somewhere to cool.
