## Ingredients
* 1¼ C. Sugar
* 2 t. Cornstarch
* 2 large Eggs
* 2 large Egg Yolks
* ½ C. Fresh Lemon Juice
* 6 T. Butter, cut into cubes
* ½ pint Heavy Whipping Cream
* 6 T. Blueberries
* Mint leaves, for garnish

## Instructions
Place the sugar and the cornstarch in a large saucepan and whisk together.<br/>
Add the 2 eggs and 2 egg yolks to the sugar and whisk well.<br/>
Add the lemon juice to the mixture and blend well.<br/>
Add the cubed butter to the mixture and place over medium heat. Whisk constantly until the butter has melted and the mixture thickens and boils, about 10 minutes.<br/>
Remove from heat and transfer to a medium bowl.<br/>
Press plastic wrap into the top of the curd to prevent a skin from forming. Chill until cold( at least 2-3 hours).<br/>
Whip the heavy cream until it holds stiff peaks. reserve 6 T. of the whipped cream for topping.<br/>
Gently fold ½ of the chilled lemon curd into the whipped cream. Place mousse in a piping bag.<br/>
Divide the remaining lemon curd among 6 smaller serving dishes or jars, spreading evenly over the bottom of each dish.<br/>
Pipe the lemon mousse on top of the lemon curd, dividing evenly amount each dish.<br/>
Top each dish of mousse with about 1 T. of the reserved whipped cream.<br/>
Place about 1 T. of blueberries on top of each whipped cream dollop.<br/>
Garnish each dish with a mint leaf.<br/>
Serve chilled.<br/>
