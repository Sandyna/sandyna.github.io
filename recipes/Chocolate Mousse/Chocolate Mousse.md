## Ingredients
* 250g dark chocolate - about 60% cocoa, e.g. Lindt 60%, Migros 70% + Edelbitter, Valrhona, Trader Joe's dark belgian chocolate...
* 250 ml (or half pint) fresh whipping cream
* 4 to 5 fresh egg whites

## Instructions
Melt the chocolate using a water bath (keep some chocolate aside if you want it crunchy, see below)<br/>
Separate egg whites from egg yolks<br/>
Whip the cream<br/>
Slowly mix chocolate into the whipped cream using a whisk<br/>
Clean the mixer. Whip the egg whites until stiff<br/>
Mix egg white into the chocolate cream using a whisk<br/>
For added crunch, mix some ground chocolate into the mousse<br/>
Refrigerate<br/>
This recipe uses raw egg, caution advised.<br/>
