## Ingredients
* 1 can condensed milk (~400g)
* 2 cans/cups whipping cream (~400-500ml)
* Toppings (chocolate, biscuits, fruit, etc.)

## Instructions
Mix desired topping with condensed milk. Whip the cream and gently fold in. Decorate and freeze at least for 6 hours.<br/>
The batter can be divided into several bowls to make more kinds icecream.<br/>
