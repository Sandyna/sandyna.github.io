## Ingredients
* 1 prepared cat (skinned, disembowled)
* 250g smoked bacon
* Salt
* Ground pepper
* Flour
* 2 Onions
* Some red wine
* 2,5dl meat broth
* Some piment
* 1-2 launel leafs
* 50g gingerbread
* 3 tbsp red currant jam

## Instructions
Rinse and pat dry cat pieces, add salt and pepper and turn in flour. Cut bacon into cubes, fry them in a raosting tin and take them out. Fry the cat meat in the bacon grease, take out again. Add the peeled and cut onions to the grease, fry until glazed, add the cat meat and bacon cubes back. Deglaze with red wine, and add the broth until the meat is covered. Add some piment and launel, turn head down and let it simmer for 1h.<br/>
Grate the gingerbread into fine pieces, use it to thicken the sauce. Taste with the current jam and possibly lemon juice.<br/>
Serve with potato dumplings and red cabbage.<br/>
