## Ingredients
* 1 Cup of sugar
* 1/2 Cup of water
* 1 Tablespoon of lemon juice
* 1 Tablespoon of honey
* 1/2 Teaspoon of ground ginger
* 1/4 Teaspoon of ground cloves

## Instructions
Mix everyting in a saucepan and bring to boil.<br/>
Let simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring regularly.<br/>
Line a baking tray with baking paper. When the mixture is syrupy and thicker, let it cool and pour drops of the size of a coin on the baking paper.<br/>
Let the drops cool for 20 minutes and dust them with confectioner or dusting sugar.<br/>
