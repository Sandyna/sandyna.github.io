## Ingredients
* 4 cups milk
* 1 tablespoon sahlab (or cornstarch, use 4 tablespoons)
* 1 cup sugar
* 1 tablespoon rose water or 1 teaspoon of vanilla
* Ground cinnamon or chopped pistachios on top

## Instructions
Place the milk in a saucepan and add the sahlab previously mixed with sugar. Stir and bring to a simmer, and keep stirring until thickened. Add the rose water and stir to mix. Transfer to cups and sprinkle with cinnamon. Serve with crackers or kaak.<br/>
Note:<br/>
If the quantity of sahlab is not sufficient to thicken the mixture, add more, 1/2 tablespoon or up to one tablespoon. If unable to source the sahlab, substitute cornstarch and double the quantity (4 to 6 tablespoons cornstarch)<br/>
