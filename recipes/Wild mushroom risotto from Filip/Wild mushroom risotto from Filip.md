## Ingredients
* 1-2 Leek

### 500g-700g wild mushrooms, you can substitute dried 3:1
* 2dcl-4dcl Dry shardonay wine or other white
* Dry sherry or vermut
* Butter or olive oil
* 300g-400g arborio rice
* 3/4l broth or water
* 70g parmesan
* salt
* pepper

## Instructions
Roast and season sliced mushrooms with olive oil or butter until browned, put aside.<br/>
Roast cut leek with butter until it smells nice. Add rice and roast until it starts going soft. Add wine and cook until steamed off. Add sherry or vermut. Take a drink to Filip.<br/>
Bring broth to boil. Add the browned mushrooms and the boiling broth. Cook until it looks like risotto. Remove from heat and mix in shredded parmesan.<br/>
